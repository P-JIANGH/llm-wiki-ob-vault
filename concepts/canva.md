# Canva

[[design-automation]] | [[ai-design-tools]]

## Overview

Canva is a graphic design platform enabling creation of presentations, social media graphics, and documents. It has integrated AI features including Magic Write (AI text generation) and Magic Design (AI-assisted templates).

## Key AI Features

- **Magic Write**: AI copywriting integrated into designs
- **Magic Eraser**: Remove objects from images
- **Translate**: One-click translation of designs
- **AI to Design**: Text-to-design using AI

## Relationship to Other Projects

- Competes with [[Adobe Firefly]] and [[Figma]] in the design tool space
- Related to [[image-generation]] tools for content creation
