# Meta LLaMA

[[open-source-llm-projects]] | [[foundation-models]]

## Overview

Meta LLaMA is Meta's open-source large language model series. Starting with LLaMA 1 (2023), the family has expanded through LLaMA 2, LLaMA 3, and LLaMA 3.1, progressively increasing context length, reasoning ability, and open-source friendliness.

## Key Models

- **LLaMA 1**: 7B–65B, first open LLM comparable to GPT-3
- **LLaMA 2**: 7B–70B, open weights with commercial license
- **LLaMA 3**: 8B–70B, improved reasoning, trained on 15T tokens
- **LLaMA 3.1**: 8B–405B, largest open weights model

## Relationship to Other Projects

- Foundation for [[llama.cpp]], [[vLLM]], [[SGLang]], and most open LLM ecosystems
- Compared with [[Mistral]] and [[Qwen]] as open model families
