# Game Design Document (GDD) Template & Guide

Source: https://indiegameacademy.com/free-game-design-document-template-how-to-guide/
Date: 2026-04-22

## What is a GDD?
A written document to share vision, describe the game, and map out implementation. It's a living document that evolves with the project.

## Core Functions
1. Shares the Vision
2. Keeps the Team Aligned
3. Organizes Your Thoughts

## The One Pager
- Condensed version, maximized for readability
- Like a movie poster + quick summary
- Great for brainstorming and quick pitches

## GDD Structure
1. The Basics / Elevator Pitch (title, tagline, genre, target audience, platform, inspirations)
2. How It Plays (core loop, key actions, rules & systems, level design)
3. The Story & World (plot, characters, setting, themes)
4. The Look & Feel (visual style, environments, UI/UX)
5. What It Sounds Like (music, SFX, voices)
6. Tech Stuff (engine, tools, monetization)
7. The Plan (timeline, team roles)

## Tips
- Keep it clear & simple, shorter is often better
- Write as if you're playing it
- Focus on what matters, cut irrelevant sections
- Get a second opinion from someone unfamiliar
- Add pictures: flowcharts, sketches, mockups
