# Indie Game Marketing Research 2025

Source: https://howtomarketagame.com/2025/12/15/leftover-research-from-2025/
Date: 2026-04-22

## Key Findings

### Holiday Games Don't Sell
- 2020-2025 data: Only 1 holiday-related game earned >1000 Steam reviews (Christmas Massacre, horror)
- Gamers lack tradition of "soaking up holiday cheer via video games"
- Exception: Holiday DLC or free update for existing hit

### Pixel Art Genre Performance
- Pixel art skills are undervalued in platformers
- Perform better in crafty-buildy-strategy-simulation genres:
  - Cast n Chill (idle management fishing) — 5652 reviews
  - Super Fantasy Kingdom (top-down pixel art cities)
  - BALL x PIT (quasi-3D pixel art)

### Reddit Marketing
- "Be a redditor with a game, not a game developer that posts to reddit"
- Good contributors have community karma and participate in gaming subs
- Banned users typically have 1 karma, only post in generic subs

### Launch Timing
- December-January: little competition, industry stops launching
- Cairn demo launched before Steam Winter Sale, landed on Trending Free for 1.5 months, 500K demo downloads

### Steam Revenue Tiers
- ~$900K gross: Valve sends smaller chocolate box
- >$1M gross: La Maison du Chocolat hatbox (EU) or Frans Collection (NA)
- At $1M gross, Valve earns ~$300K

### Slow Build Case Study: Tower Factory
- 16 months dev time, solo dev
- Month 1: 6.8% conversion, 329 reviews
- Year 1: ~1000 reviews
- 329 first-month reviews is lowest starting spike while still reaching "Real Steam"
